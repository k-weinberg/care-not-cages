A big part of our work is speaking directly with people affected by Monroe County’s criminal legal system, gathering individual stories and data about what’s working, and what’s not.<\/em><\/p>\n\n\n\n

It’s very expensive to be ‘on paper’ \u2014 so many Monroe County community members spend thousands of dollars a year on court-ordered drug tests, electronic monitoring fees, and other court expenses \u2014 not to mention lost jobs and ruined careers from a criminal record or the jail time itself.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
